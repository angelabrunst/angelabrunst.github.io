Target Audience

The audience will be those who are looking for the weather in their area. Since we are targeting outdoor adventurers, the audience viewing the site will be between the ages of 18 to 40. The site will target all marital statuses and have a section for family friendly hikes. The sight will mostly be viewed via mobile devices since the audience will rely on their phones for most information.

Personas

Richard is a 30 year old male looking for possible mountain biking trails near Preston, ID. He is working a mid-level corporate job making $60,000 per year with a degree in Finance. He is visiting relatives in the area and looking for a brief adventure while in town. He came to the The Weather Rock because it offered both information on the weather during his trip and trail options.

Monica is a 42 year old female in looking at options for a family reunion near Bear Lake. She is a mother of three teenagers and a high school graduate with an income of $45,000. She was interested in Fish Haven and interested in the weather for that week along with live images of the area.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A student at USU is looking at mountain biking trails near Preston, ID. They are drawn to the site because of the image gallery. They are then drawn to the weather feature to plan their trip.

Scenario 2: A youth group party is held at Bear Lake. The leader of the group party notices a storm rolling in and sends a report through the weather watch page. The leader also posts images of the area.

Scenario 3: A young family in Soda Springs is looking for an easy family hike in the area. They are drawn to the hiking information on the site and are able to plan when to go due to the weather feature.

Scenario 4: A millennial is looking up the home of Napoleon Dynamite and is drawn to more information about the town of Preston, ID. They look through the images posted in the gallery.

Scenario 5: A soon-to-be college graduate is considering accepting a job offer located in Soda Springs, ID. He is looking to see if the area can support his desire to maintain an active outdoor lifestyle. He looks through the hiking information and the current weather.
