Target Audience

The audience will be those who are looking for the weather in their area. Since we are targeting outdoor adventurers, the audience viewing the site will be between the ages of 18 to 40. The site will target all marital statuses and have a section for family friendly hikes. The sight will mostly be viewed via mobile devices since the audience will rely on their phones for most information.

Personas

Richard is a 30 year old male looking for possible mountain biking trails near Preston, ID. He is working a mid-level corporate job making $60,000 per year with a degree in Finance. He is visiting relatives in the area and looking for a brief adventure while in town. He came to the The Weather Rock because it offered both information on the weather during his trip and trail options.

Monica is a 42 year old female in looking at options for a family reunion near Bear Lake. She is a mother of three teenagers and a high school graduate with an income of $45,000. She was interested in Fish Haven and interested in the weather for that week along with live images of the area.

Scenarios

The Weather Rock will be a source for both weather and information about various locations in Idaho. While people are looking for hikes in those various areas they will be routed to the site that will help completely plan their outdoor adventure.
